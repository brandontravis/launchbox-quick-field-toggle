### Installation

1. Download the latest release
2. Copy `QuickFieldToggle.dll` and `quickfieldtoggle.sample.json` to your `Plugins` folder
3. Rename `quickfieldtoggle.sample.json` to `quickfieldtoggle.json`
4. Restart LaunchBox
5. Right-click any game and enjoy!
